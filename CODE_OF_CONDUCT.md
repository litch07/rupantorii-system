# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Assume good intentions.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostility.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer
through the repository or via the public contact channels listed in README.

We will review and address issues promptly and fairly.
